The back parking lots of the school will be the only area in which those attending events hosted at the gym can park. Parking anywhere else will likley result in towing.

Security measures

Here at Westside, we pride ourselves in being a safe and accepting school. Bags will be checked upon arrival. weapons of any kind are banned from all events. If we find a weapon in your bag, you are subject to a fine and/or arrest. Depending on the event, police may be stationed outside the school.
